About Our Performers

The International Theatre Festival brings together six celebrated theatre groups and organizations from across Europe:

Natyadhara is a classical and contemporary theatre ensemble known for blending traditional storytelling with modern stagecraft. Their performances celebrate the richness of classical Indian theatre traditions while engaging contemporary audiences.

Stichting Sanskriti & Sanskar is the organizing foundation, dedicated to promoting culture, heritage, and arts in the Netherlands. They also present performances and cultural programs throughout the year.

Majlish creates powerful contemporary theatre productions with a strong community focus. They are recognized for their innovative approaches to theatrical narrative and their commitment to making theatre accessible to diverse audiences across the Netherlands.

Abhivyakti is a performing arts group dedicated to promoting Gujarati theatre and culture. Through their performances, they preserve and celebrate Gujarati heritage while creating bridges between traditional and modern artistic expressions.

Rocky Amaretto is an independent theatre artist bringing unique solo and collaborative performances. Known for experimental and boundary-pushing theatre, Rocky Amaretto adds an eclectic energy to the festival lineup.
