International Theatre Festival 2026 — Hoofddorp

Date: 25 January 2026   |   Venue: Cpunt Theatre, Raadhuisplein 3, 2132 TZ Hoofddorp   |   Hosted by: Stichting Sanskriti & Sanskar

Premium international theatre performances featuring acclaimed theatre groups from across Europe.

Welcome to the International Theatre Festival 2026 — the premier international theatre festival bringing together the finest theatre companies and performers from across Europe and beyond. Hosted in Hoofddorp (near Amsterdam), this international theatre festival features contemporary and classical theatre performances that celebrate the art of performing.

The International Theatre Festival in the Netherlands showcases powerful storytelling, inventive staging, and a celebration of international performing arts. This festival is a must-attend for lovers of live theatre in and around Amsterdam.

About the Organizer

Organized by Stichting Sanskriti & Sanskar — an organization dedicated to promoting culture, heritage and arts within the Netherlands.

About Our Performers

The International Theatre Festival brings together celebrated theatre groups and organizations from across Europe:

Natyadhara is a classical and contemporary theatre ensemble known for blending traditional storytelling with modern stagecraft.

Stichting Sanskriti & Sanskar is the organizing foundation, dedicated to promoting culture, heritage, and arts in the Netherlands.

Majlish was founded in the beginning of 2016 when a group of friends with intent and passion for Bengali drama decided to form a team and wanted to produce Bengali plays regularly on stage. This has led to the formation of MAJLISH. Since then, MAJLISH has grown rapidly with increasingly active member-base and besides Kolkata, has extended its activities to the Netherlands and the UK as well. Let's come and watch Majlish as they bring a vibrant contemporary Bengali play on stage.

Fragments is an English Improvised Theatre group based out of Utrecht. Let's encourage Fragments as they bring a unique blend of emotions with their improvised drama.

Theatergroep Rocky Amaretto speelt geïmproviseerd theater in Utrecht. Acht acteurs/makers/schrijvers. Laten we een uniek, geïmproviseerd verhaal over broers en zussen bekijken, gepresenteerd door Rocky Amaretto.

Abhivyakti is a performing arts group dedicated to promoting Gujarati theatre and culture.
