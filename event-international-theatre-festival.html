About Our Performers

The International Theatre Festival brings together celebrated theatre groups and organizations from across Europe:

Natyadhara is a classical and contemporary theatre ensemble known for blending traditional storytelling with modern stagecraft.

Stichting Sanskriti & Sanskar is the organizing foundation, dedicated to promoting culture, heritage, and arts in the Netherlands.

Majlish was founded in the beginning of 2016 when a group of friends with intent and passion for Bengali drama decided to form a team and wanted to produce Bengali plays regularly on stage. This has led to the formation of MAJLISH. Since then, MAJLISH has grown rapidly with increasingly active member-base and besides Kolkata, has extended its activities to the Netherlands and the UK as well. Let's come and watch Majlish as they bring a vibrant contemporary Bengali play on stage.

Fragments is an English Improvised Theatre group based out of Utrecht. Let's encourage Fragments as they bring a unique blend of emotions with their improvised drama.

Abhivyakti is a passionate ensemble of Gujarati artists ‚Äî professionals, students, and dreamers ‚Äî united by their love for theatre. With a shared mission to celebrate Gujarat's cultural heritage, they bring Gujarat's traditions and spirit to life through drama, music, humour, and heartfelt performances. Known for their relatable comedy, their popular works include "Besana Ma Panchat", "Saurashtra Mail" and "Mathe Padyo Intern". Their Gujarati adaptation of "Panchamved" was showcased under the prestigious banner of India's National School of Drama (NSD).
