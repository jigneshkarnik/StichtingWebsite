'Bhartiya First' Conclave: Foundations for Collaboration

By Admin October 4, 2025 Press Release

Wassenaar: In a landmark event that bridged communities and fostered dialogue, the 'Bhartiya First' Conclave 2025 brought together the Indian diaspora and leading Dutch personalities to lay the foundations for future collaboration.

The event, held in the picturesque town of Wassenaar, was marked by insightful discussions, cultural exchanges, and a shared commitment to strengthening the ties between the Indian community and the wider Dutch society.

"This conclave represents a significant step forward in our mission to not only preserve our heritage but to integrate meaningfully with the society we live in," remarked a spokesperson for Sanskriti & Sanskar.

Key Highlights

Several key topics were discussed during the conclave, including:

  • Strategies for better integration of Indian expatriates.
  • Promoting Indian arts and culture in the Netherlands.
  • Collaborative opportunities in business and education.

The event concluded with a networking session where attendees had the opportunity to connect with community leaders and discuss future initiatives. The success of the Bhartiya First Conclave serves as a testament to the vibrant spirit of the community.
